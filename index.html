FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need to know anything technical?

Not at all. We handle everything — design, code, hosting setup, and domain. You just need to tell us about your business.

How long does it take?

Most websites are ready within 5–10 working days depending on the plan and how quickly you can share feedback.

Will my website work on mobile?

Yes, every website we build is fully responsive and tested on phones, tablets, and all screen sizes.

Can I update the website myself later?

Absolutely. We build on easy-to-manage systems and provide a walkthrough so you can update content on your own.

What if I need changes after launch?

Minor edits are covered for 30 days after launch. After that, we offer affordable monthly support packages.

Do you offer hosting and domain?

Yes, hosting for the first year is included in the Business and Pro plans. We also help you get your domain name.
