FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before we start.

Do I need to know anything technical?

Not at all. We handle everything — design, code, hosting, and domain. You just tell us about your business.

How long does it take?

Most websites launch within 5–10 working days, depending on the plan and feedback speed.

Will it work perfectly on mobile?

Yes, every site is fully responsive and tested across phones, tablets, and all screen sizes.

Can I make updates myself?

Absolutely. We build on easy-to-manage systems and walk you through editing your own content.

What about changes after launch?

Minor edits are free for 30 days. After that we offer affordable monthly support packages.

Is hosting and domain included?

Hosting for the first year is included in Business and Pro plans. We help with domain setup too.
